Blind Cleaning

Boldt Brothers specializes in restoring your blinds to “like-new” condition with the most effective
cleaning techniques available today. Our unique cleansing process will not void any manufacturer’s
warranties and we offer pick-up and delivery services and/or on-site cleaning.
In addition to improving the overall appearance of your workspace, this process will also
reduce dust and airborne particles in your office – a major cause of allergies and other health-related
issues. From vertical to Venetian blinds, we utilize the latest technologies for your
specific window treatment needs.

Marble Care

Natural stone offers a beautiful touch to your office environment, but it requires special care and cleaning to maintain its
original beauty. Keeping marble and stone free of dust and dry, sandy soil will minimize the wear-patterns and
scratches that can develop from everyday use. The professionals at Boldt Brothers have the necessary technology and proven
techniques to clean your marble and stone paths and floors, while also protecting them against oil and water-based stains. Our
experienced teams maintain the beauty of your marble and stone, applying only proven cleaning
techniques that will help you get to the most from your investment.
